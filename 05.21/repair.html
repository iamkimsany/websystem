Consultation

If you notice that your vision is deteriorating or you experience other eye problems, you should consult an ophthalmologist. An ophthalmologist is a doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating eye diseases. They can check your vision, detect vision abnormalities such as myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, and other eye conditions, and prescribe the necessary treatment. Treatment may include wearing glasses or contact lenses, using eye drops, undergoing hardware treatments, and other recommendations. Check the suitable date and make reservation without registration!
