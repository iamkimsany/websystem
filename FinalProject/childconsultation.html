Childern's Consultation

We also care about your children's eye health. If you notice that your child's vision is deteriorating or they experience other eye problems, you should consult an ophthalmologist. An ophthalmologist is a doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating eye diseases. They can check your child's vision, detect vision abnormalities such as myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, and other eye conditions, and prescribe the necessary treatment. Treatment may include wearing glasses or contact lenses, using eye drops, undergoing hardware treatments, and other recommendations.
